Comprehensive Financial Services

The range of services covers all financial needs, from tax filings to corporate compliance and audits. Expert support is provided across key areas to ensure accuracy, compliance, and peace of mind.

Income Tax & TDS

  • ITR Filing: Preparation and e-filing of Income Tax Returns for individuals and businesses.
  • TDS Filing: Filing of TDS returns to ensure compliance.
  • Trust 12A & 80G: Assistance with registration and compliance for trusts under Sections 12A and 80G.
  • Tax Audit Support: Support to prepare for and navigate tax audits.
  • Tax Consultant: Tax planning and advisory to minimize liabilities and maximize savings.

GST

  • GST Registration: Simplified process for obtaining GST registration.
  • GST Return Filings: Filing of all GSTRs.
  • GST Audit: Support for GST audits.
  • GST ITC Reconciliation: Reconciliation of Input Tax Credit to maximize benefits.
  • Handling Notices: Handling of GST notices and compliance issues.

Company Law & LLP

  • Company & LLP Incorporation: Services for the incorporation of a company or Limited Liability Partnership.
  • Annual Filings: Filing of all annual returns and required forms with the ROC/MCA.
  • ROC/MCA Compliances: Management of all regulatory requirements.

Accounting & Auditing

  • Complete Accounting: Accounting services, from transaction recording to financial statement preparation.
  • Book Keeping: Record-keeping to maintain financial accuracy.
  • Internal Audits: Independent internal audits to enhance controls and identify inefficiencies.
  • Stock Audits: Physical verification and valuation of inventory.
  • PAN, TAN, IEC: Application and management for Permanent Account Numbers, Tax Deduction Account Numbers, and Import Export Codes.
  • Digital Signature Certificate (DSC): Guidance and processing for obtaining and renewing Digital Signature Certificates.

Frequently Asked Questions

What accounting services do you offer? +

We offer a comprehensive range of accounting services including bookkeeping, financial statement preparation, tax filing, GST compliance, company incorporation, auditing, and more. Please see our Services page for complete details.

How do you charge for your services? +

Our fees depend on the scope and complexity of the services required. We offer both project-based pricing and ongoing retainer arrangements. Contact us for a personalized quote based on your specific needs.

What information do I need to provide for tax filing? +

Typically, you'll need to provide documents such as Form 16, bank statements, investment proofs, rent receipts (if applicable), home loan statements, and details of any other income sources. We provide a comprehensive checklist once you engage our services.

How long does it take to incorporate a company? +

The timeline for company incorporation can vary but typically takes between 10-15 working days, depending on the documentation provided and government processing times. We streamline the process to make it as efficient as possible.

Do you handle GST registration for businesses outside Chennai? +

Yes, we provide GST registration and compliance services for businesses across India. Our digital processes allow us to efficiently serve clients regardless of location.
